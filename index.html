Camel

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of their specially designed feet
  3. When they find water they drink as much as possible

Chameleon

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Pigeon

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredible complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Teddy Bear

Interesting Facts about Bears

  1. Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals
  2. Bears grieve deeply for others
  3. Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
  4. Bears care deeply about family members

Panda

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day
  4. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Flying Cicada

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicadas

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs. They are about the size of a grain of rice when they drop from a tree branch to the ground and start digging.
  2. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus. (Females make a much quieter sound by flicking their wings.)
  3. Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.
  4. They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.
